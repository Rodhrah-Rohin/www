## **3. Typography**

* **Primary Typeface (Headings):** *Cinzel* or *Playfair Display*.
* *Why:* These fonts have a "chiseled" look that feels both ancient and prestigious.

* **Secondary Typeface (Body):** *Montserrat* or *Inter*.
* *Why:* Clean, geometric sans-serifs that represent modern "Pragati" (progress) and readability.

* **Sanskrit Script:** *Noto Sans Devanagari*.
* *Why:* Clean and legible, ensuring the tagline maintains its sacred weight without looking cluttered.
